Explore Our World-Class Facilities

Bio-Tech Park provides top-tier infrastructure and support to advance biotechnology research and innovation. Our facilities cater to both students and researchers, enabling high-quality learning, experimentation, and collaboration.

Advanced Laboratories

Our advanced laboratories offer a sophisticated environment for molecular biology, microbiology, and chemical analysis. Equipped with cutting-edge instruments, they provide precision and safety for both academic and industrial research.

Research Library

The research library hosts extensive books, journals, and digital resources on biotechnology, genetics, and bioinformatics. It offers a quiet and well-organized space for focused research and study.

Innovation Center

The Innovation Center supports startups and students through mentorship, lab access, and guidance for funding and commercialization. It bridges academic research with real-world applications.

Conference Halls

Our modern conference halls feature projectors, audio systems, and flexible seating for seminars, workshops, and meetings — encouraging collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria offers healthy meals and a relaxing environment, fostering informal discussions and networking among students and researchers.

Outdoor Spaces

Beautifully landscaped outdoor areas provide a calm environment for relaxation, brainstorming, and collaboration — enhancing well-being and creativity.
